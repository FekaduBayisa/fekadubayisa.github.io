Welcome!

I am a Research Fellow at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, University of Guelph, Canada. Previous positions include Postdoctoral Researcher roles at Auburn University, USA, 2021–2023, University of Oslo, Norway, 2020–2021, and Umeå University, Sweden, 2018–2020.

I received a Ph.D. in Mathematical Statistics from Umeå University, Sweden, in June 2018.

My research interests include Statistical Machine Learning, Data Science, Mathematical Statistics, Computational Statistics, and High-dimensional Inference.
